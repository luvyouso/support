FAQ


I sent notification to locate my phone, now what?

Check your email to see if you received any responses from Find My Phone.

I have not received any emails from Find My Phone, now what?

There are several reasons for why this may happen.

  • Your phone may be out of battery or not have a data connection, in which case it will not respond.
  • Sometimes notifications can take time to get to the phone. Give it up to 30 minutes.
  • Try texting the different triggers to your phone. By default, that is 01234 to locate, and 56789 to ring.
  • Lastly, try other methods/apps, like the Android Device Manager

What is my username?

Username is the email address you registered with Find My Phone on your device. If you did not register from inside the app, then you do not have a web account. Use the SMS triggers instead (Hint: text 01234 to your lost phone).
